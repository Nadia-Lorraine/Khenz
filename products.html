Offers

Khenz’s new digital ticketing infrastructure seamlessly facilitates bus ticket sales across diverse sales channels. The technology allows end users to book bus reservations and payment up to a week or more prior to travel, On Point of Sale, online, using Android or iOS mobile apps, or via USSD. The solution provides major benefits to passengers who would otherwise arrive hours ahead of departure, hopeful to arrange a ticket.


This technology also provides bus companies with a single consolidated platform to track customer trends as well as trace individual purchasing patterns over time, all compiled through a single database for real-time analysis. Khenz’s “precious goods” technology – checking tickets upon entering and exiting the bus – tracks passengers across the country.

Khenz provides digital ticketing platforms that enable quick and secure access to regional bus tickets. This same technology works to track “precious goods” – young women – moving from one point to another via public transport, thus enhancing traveler safety on the road.
