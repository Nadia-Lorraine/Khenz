Why starting

with Rwanda

Only 20 years out of one of the most devastating genocides in history, Rwanda has made incredible strides. It is ranked 3rd in Africa by the World Bank’s 2015 Ease of Doing Business Index – a recognition largely due to President Kagame’s “Vision 2020,” which is promoting industry and investment to transform Rwanda into a modern middle-income country. Over the last 10 years, Rwanda has enjoyed an average annual GDP growth rate of 7.7%, compared to the Sub-Saharan Africa average of 4.8%..

  • Target market for customers

    Khenz’s target market is the young, urban, middle-class who are increasingly mobile, frequently moving across Rwanda for work, school, and visiting.

  • Competitor Landscape

    New entrants in the electronic ticketing space in Rwanda lack the multi-channel sales options which have enabled Khenz to increase customer accessibility

  • investment opportunity

    Khenz technology replaces physical transaction management systems for bus companies, and thus speeds up standard business procedures and controls operations more effectively.
