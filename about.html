ABOUT

KHENZ LTDWe create awesome stuff

Khenz Ltd is a Rwandan software company that fosters safety and convenience for travellers as a trusted, intelligent partner. We provide a digital ticketing platform that enables a quick and secure access to bus tickets for long distance Bus Operators. The same platform allows ensuring traveler’s safety on roads and tracking precious goods moving from one point to another in the public transport. The platform is available on different sales channels such as mobile (USSD,IOS and Android), online, next shop and bus premises.
